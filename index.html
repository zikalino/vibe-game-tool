Tile Water Sandbox

Move with Arrow Keys or WASD. Break soil in front of you with Space.

Press F to pour water where your facing arrow points.

With Water tool selected, hold left mouse to pour from your cursor.

Stone is solid, soil is breakable, and water flows down first then spreads sideways.

Mouse build mode: left click places selected tile, right click erases.

Resize the tile grid by dragging its bottom-right corner; this changes map rows/columns (up to 1000x1000 tiles).
